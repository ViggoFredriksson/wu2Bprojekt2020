About track cycling

What is this website?

Hello and welcome to to this website. This website exist to introduce people to the sport of track cycling. With the 2020 Summer Olympics coming up, I, the creator of this website, thought it would be nice if there was a place where people without much previous knowledge of track cycling could get an introduction to the sport.
